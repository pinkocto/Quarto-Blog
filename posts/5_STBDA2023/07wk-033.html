4. 분석

- 분석0: 선형회귀

lr = sklearn.linear_model.LinearRegression()
lr.fit(X,y)
df_train['employment_score_hat'] = lr.predict(X)
df_test['employment_score_hat'] = lr.predict(XX)
print(f'train score: {lr.score(X,y):.4f}')
print(f'test score: {lr.score(XX,yy):.4f}')
